registration system


Hi! This is the system where you can register for SDU campus events. It's simple – we'll explain everything now.



1. Create an account 2. Tell us about yourself 3. Choose an event
Click “register” and log in to the system using your email You’ll need to fill out a small form – tell us your name, where you study, and how we can contact you. We use this information to communicate with event participants and to generate certificates On the registration page there’s a list of events you can join. Use the filters and choose the ones you're truly interested in!
4. Set your schedule 5. Download QR 6. Download certificate
Adjust your personal schedule – quickly choose the most interesting events before all the slots are taken! Download QR codes for events and use them to enter After each event, in the “My Schedule” section, you can download a certificate of attendance – don’t forget to scan the QR upon entry so your attendance is recorded
