Help

Hi! Here we’ll try to answer all your questions about using the system. If you still have questions — write to us through the feedback form, we’ll definitely help.

What is this?

This is a special system for events at the SDU. Some time before an event, the organizers open registration — and the upcoming event appears in your personal account, where you can sign up. The system is used to manage each participant’s schedule, check in at events, and download attendance certificates. Register — and you’ll see everything.

How to register?

There is a "register" button in the top panel. On the page you land on, there will be a field to enter your email. It’s important to enter a real email — you’ll need it to access your account. We will send a generated password to your email. Go to the login page ("log in") and log in using your email and the received password. Want to change your password? You’ll be able to do that in your profile after registration.

Why do I need to agree to photography?

We try to take photos at all events — so you’ll have memories, and we can show others how it was. For this, we need your consent. Next to the consent checkbox, there’s a link to download the document — you can read it in more detail there.

I’m already registered. How do I access my account?

Look a little higher — there is a "log in" button. On the login page, you’ll need to enter the email you used during registration and your password. One more click — and you’re in your account.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

There is a button for this case on the login page. Click it — and you’ll get to the password recovery page. You’ll need to enter your email again, and we’ll generate a new password and send it to you. A few moments — and you can log in again.

There’s no answer to my question

We’ve tried to cover everything important for users who haven’t logged in yet. If your question is about registering for an event, your schedule, or your account — log in and read the help section there, you’ll likely find all the answers. If not — write to us through the feedback form, we’ll help!
