Help

Hi! Here we will try to answer all your questions about using the system. If you still have questions – contact us through the feedback form and we will definitely help.

What is this?

If you're here, you probably already know – but just in case. This is a special system for events of SDU. Some time before the event, organizers open registration – and the upcoming event appears in your account, where you can sign up. The system is used to manage each participant's schedule, check in to events, and download attendance certificates.

How to register for an event?

In the top panel, there's an "Events" button. On the page you land on, you’ll see a list of all events currently open for registration. If there are too many – use filters on the left side to find what suits you best. Hover over an event name to see a short description. Like it? Click “I want to go” and create your schedule by selecting only the activities you’re most interested in.

I can’t fit everything I want into my schedule

During some sessions, events run in parallel – you can only choose one. Some fill up fast – and registration closes. Take a look – maybe the parallel event is also interesting?

I registered but can’t find my schedule

In the same top panel, there’s a "My Schedule" section. You can view your registrations there and download your schedule as a PDF. You can also change your schedule if you want.

I can't attend. What should I do?

On the “My Schedule” page, you can cancel your registration. Please do so if you can't come – someone else might want to attend but there are no spots left.

What is the QR for?

We use QR codes for checking in at events. Organizers scan them and the system marks your attendance. Each event in your schedule has its own QR code. Click the “QR” button next to the event on the “My Schedule” page to download it.

Where can I find the attendance certificate?

At the bottom of the “My Schedule” page, you’ll find a list of events you’ve attended. Next to each is a button to download your certificate. To receive a certificate, make sure you’ve filled in your personal profile info and presented your QR code at the entrance.

My name doesn’t appear on the certificate, why?

To create your certificate properly, you must fill out your profile information. Make sure it’s saved – then download the certificate again. That should fix it.

How can I change my password?

You can change your password on your profile page. You’ll need to enter your current password and create a new one. After changing it, you’ll need to log in again.

How can I change my email?

Currently, all accounts are linked to an email address, and it cannot be changed. If you lose access to your email or stop using it – it's better to just create a new account before your next registration.

More questions?

Still have questions or problems with the system? Write to us via the feedback form — we’ll help! The link is in the top panel.
